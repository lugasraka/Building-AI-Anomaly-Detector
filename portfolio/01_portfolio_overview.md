## Executive Summary

The AI Solutions Portfolio represents NexusCorp's strategic investment in AI-powered energy optimization for the Smart Energy Division. This portfolio of 4 interconnected AI projects delivers measurable energy savings, operational efficiency, and sustainability impact while advancing the IntelliCore Vision platform roadmap.

**Portfolio Investment:** $650,000 (FY2026)  
**Expected ROI:** 165% over 3 years  
**Target Impact:** $2.5M annual energy savings, 2,000 tons CO2 reduction  
**Team Size:** 6 FTEs (AI Engineers, MLOps, Data Scientist)  
**Timeline:** 18-month rollout (Q1 2026 - Q2 2027)
