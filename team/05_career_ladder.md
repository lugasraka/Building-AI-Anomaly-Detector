# AI Team Career Ladder
## Career Progression Framework & Promotion Criteria

**Document Version:** 1.0  
**Last Updated:** February 2026  
**Purpose:** Define clear career paths and promotion criteria for AI team members

---

## Career Progression Philosophy

**Core Principles:**
1. **Impact Over Title:** Advancement based on impact, not just tenure
2. **Multiple Paths:** Technical (IC) and Management tracks available
3. **Clear Criteria:** Transparent expectations at each level
4. **Continuous Growth:** Regular feedback and development opportunities
5. **Merit-Based:** Promotions earned through demonstrated performance

**Career Tracks:**
- **Technical Track:** Individual Contributor (IC) path focusing on deep expertise
- **Management Track:** People leadership path focusing on team and organizational impact
- **Hybrid Track:** Combination of technical depth and leadership (Staff+ levels)

---

## Technical Track: Individual Contributor (IC)

### Level 1: Junior AI Engineer
**Experience:** 0-2 years  
**Salary Range:** SGD 60,000 - 80,000

**Expectations:**
- **Technical:** Basic proficiency in Python and ML fundamentals
- **Scope:** Works on well-defined tasks with supervision
- **Impact:** Delivers small features or bug fixes
- **Collaboration:** Learns from peers, asks questions
- **Growth:** Rapidly building technical skills

**Promotion Criteria to Level 2:**
- [ ] 12-18 months in role
- [ ] Can work independently on standard tasks
- [ ] Delivers 3+ features independently
- [ ] Code quality meets team standards
- [ ] Demonstrates understanding of system architecture
- [ ] Positive peer feedback on collaboration

---

### Level 2: AI Engineer I (Mid-Level)
**Experience:** 2-4 years  
**Salary Range:** SGD 80,000 - 110,000

**Expectations:**
- **Technical:** Solid ML engineering skills, understands algorithms
- **Scope:** Owns features end-to-end with minimal guidance
- **Impact:** Delivers medium-complexity features regularly
- **Collaboration:** Participates in code reviews, helps junior engineers
- **Growth:** Developing specialization in one area

**Key Responsibilities:**
- Implement ML models and features independently
- Write comprehensive tests and documentation
- Participate in architecture discussions
- Mentor junior engineers (informally)
- Contribute to team processes and best practices

**Promotion Criteria to Level 3:**
- [ ] 18-24 months in role (or 3-4 years total experience)
- [ ] Leads technical design for small projects
- [ ] Delivers complex features with high quality
- [ ] Mentors 1+ junior engineers to success
- [ ] Contributes to team technical standards
- [ ] Recognized as "go-to" person for specific technology/domain
- [ ] Presents at team Lunch & Learn or external events

---

### Level 3: AI Engineer II (Senior)
**Experience:** 4-7 years  
**Salary Range:** SGD 120,000 - 160,000

**Expectations:**
- **Technical:** Expert in ML engineering, system design
- **Scope:** Leads technical design for major components
- **Impact:** Drives significant technical decisions and innovations
- **Collaboration:** Formal mentorship, cross-functional leadership
- **Growth:** Expanding influence beyond immediate team

**Key Responsibilities:**
- Lead architecture and design for complex projects
- Drive technical decisions with long-term impact
- Mentor 2+ engineers (formal mentorship)
- Define team technical standards and best practices
- Contribute to hiring and interviewing
- Represent team in cross-functional initiatives

**Promotion Criteria to Level 4:**
- [ ] 24-36 months in role (or 6-8 years total experience)
- [ ] Leads architecture for multiple major projects
- [ ] Drives innovation (new algorithms, significant optimizations)
- [ ] Successfully mentors 2+ engineers to promotion
- [ ] Recognized technical authority in organization
- [ ] External recognition (conference talks, publications, patents)
- [ ] Business impact: $500K+ measurable value delivered

---

### Level 4: Staff AI Engineer
**Experience:** 7-10 years  
**Salary Range:** SGD 170,000 - 220,000

**Expectations:**
- **Technical:** Recognized expert, drives technical strategy
- **Scope:** Influences technical direction across teams/portfolio
- **Impact:** Strategic technical decisions with organization-wide impact
- **Collaboration:** Leads cross-team initiatives, external partnerships
- **Growth:** Industry thought leader, drives innovation agenda

**Key Responsibilities:**
- Define technical strategy and roadmap for AI portfolio
- Lead complex, multi-quarter technical initiatives
- Drive adoption of new technologies and methodologies
- Build and maintain external technical relationships
- Contribute to company-wide technical standards
- Hire and develop senior technical talent

**Promotion Criteria to Level 5:**
- [ ] 36-48 months in role (or 10+ years total experience)
- [ ] Industry-recognized expert (keynote speaker, published author)
- [ ] Drives major technical transformation or innovation
- [ ] Business impact: $2M+ measurable value
- [ ] Builds and leads high-performing technical teams
- [ ] Strategic advisor to executive leadership

---

### Level 5: Principal AI Engineer
**Experience:** 10+ years  
**Salary Range:** SGD 220,000 - 300,000

**Expectations:**
- **Technical:** Industry thought leader, defines best practices
- **Scope:** Company-wide technical strategy and vision
- **Impact:** Transformational impact on business and industry
- **Collaboration:** Executive-level partnerships, external influence
- **Growth:** Shapes industry direction, builds lasting legacy

**Key Responsibilities:**
- Set technical vision and strategy for Digital Innovation
- Drive breakthrough innovations and research
- Represent company at highest industry levels
- Build and scale technical organizations
- Advise C-suite on technology and AI strategy
- Create lasting technical assets (platforms, frameworks)

**Next Steps:**
- Fellow/ Distinguished Engineer (Level 6)

## Management Track: People Leaders

### Level 1: Tech Lead / Senior Engineer (Hybrid)
**Experience:** 4-6 years  
**Salary Range:** SGD 130,000 - 170,000

**Expectations:**
- **Technical:** Strong technical skills + leadership potential
- **Scope:** Leads small team (2-3 engineers) while remaining hands-on
- **Impact:** Delivers projects + develops team members
- **Collaboration:** Bridges technical and business needs
- **Growth:** Testing management aptitude while maintaining technical depth

**Key Responsibilities:**
- Lead technical projects and small teams
- Mentor and develop junior engineers
- Participate in hiring and interviewing
- Contribute to sprint planning and estimation
- Balance 70% technical / 30% leadership work

**Transition Criteria to Management:**
- [ ] Demonstrated people leadership skills
- [ ] Successfully mentored 2+ engineers
- [ ] Interest in management career path
- [ ] Strong communication and stakeholder management
- [ ] Business acumen and strategic thinking

---

### Level 2: Engineering Manager
**Experience:** 6-9 years  
**Salary Range:** SGD 160,000 - 200,000

**Expectations:**
- **Management:** First-line manager, manages 4-8 engineers
- **Scope:** Owns team delivery and performance
- **Impact:** Team productivity, quality, and growth
- **Collaboration:** Cross-functional leadership, stakeholder management
- **Growth:** Developing broader organizational impact

**Key Responsibilities:**
- Manage team of 4-8 AI engineers
- Own sprint/team delivery and velocity
- Conduct performance reviews and 1:1s
- Hire and onboard new team members
- Manage team budget and resources
- Resolve conflicts and blockers
- Balance 30% technical / 70% management

**Promotion Criteria to Level 3:**
- [ ] 24-36 months in role
- [ ] Successfully manages 6+ person team
- [ ] Delivers consistent team performance (>90% on-time)
- [ ] Promotes 2+ engineers to next level
- [ ] Manages cross-functional initiatives
- [ ] Contributes to organizational process improvements

---

### Level 3: Senior Engineering Manager
**Experience:** 9-12 years  
**Salary Range:** SGD 200,000 - 260,000

**Expectations:**
- **Management:** Manages multiple teams or large team (15+ people)
- **Scope:** Portfolio or product area ownership
- **Impact:** Strategic delivery and organizational effectiveness
- **Collaboration:** Executive-level partnerships
- **Growth:** Shaping organization culture and capabilities

**Key Responsibilities:**
- Manage 2-3 teams or 15+ engineers
- Own portfolio delivery and business outcomes
- Define hiring strategy and team structure
- Drive organizational process improvements
- Manage $2M+ budget

**Next Steps:**

## Promotion Process

### Timing & Frequency
- **Eligible:** After 12 months in current level (minimum)
- **Typical:** 18-36 months between promotions
- **Process:** Quarterly promotion cycles (Q1, Q2, Q3, Q4)
- **Exceptions:** Accelerated promotion for exceptional performance

### Promotion Package
**Manager prepares:**
1. **Promotion Justification Document**
   - Current level and time in role
   - Evidence of meeting next level criteria
   - Specific examples of impact and growth
   - Peer feedback and testimonials
   - Business case for promotion

2. **360-Degree Feedback Collection**
   - Feedback from 5-8 peers and stakeholders
   - Self-assessment from candidate
   - Manager assessment and recommendation

3. **Impact Documentation**
   - Quantified business impact (ROI, savings, revenue)
   - Technical contributions (designs, innovations)
   - Leadership impact (mentorship, team development)
   - External recognition (talks, publications)

### Review Process
1. **Manager Review:** Hiring manager submits package
2. **Peer Calibration:** Calibration session with peer managers
3. **Leadership Review:** Director/VP level review
4. **HR Review:** Compensation and level alignment
5. **Approval:** Final approval from VP or higher
6. **Communication:** Offer and celebration

### Promotion Compensation
- **Salary Increase:** 10-20% (depending on level and performance)
- **Equity Refresh:** Additional stock options/grants
- **Benefits:** Enhanced benefits at senior levels
- **Title:** New title and role designation
- **Responsibilities:** Expanded scope and expectations

## Transitioning Between Tracks

### IC to Management
**Typical Path:**
1. **Senior Engineer (L3)** → **Tech Lead** (hybrid role, 12-18 months)
2. **Tech Lead** → **Engineering Manager (L2)** (formal transition)

**Requirements:**
- Demonstrated people leadership (mentorship, coaching)
- Interest in management career
- Strong communication and stakeholder management
- Business acumen
- Willingness to reduce hands-on coding (30% technical max)

**Support Provided:**
- Management training program
- Executive coaching
- Peer manager mentorship
- Gradual transition (hybrid role first)

### Management to IC
**Typical Path:**
- **Engineering Manager** → **Staff Engineer (L4)** or **Principal Engineer (L5)**

**Requirements:**
- Deep technical skills maintained
- Desire to return to hands-on work
- Technical contributions during management tenure
- Staff/Principal level competencies

**Considerations:**
- May require "re-proving" technical skills
- Compensation adjustment (may decrease)
- Title and scope change
- Support for technical skill refresh

### Hybrid Track
**Best of Both Worlds:**
- Staff+ levels allow technical depth + leadership
- 50/50 split between technical and organizational work
- Ideal for those who want both impact and hands-on work
- Available at L4 (Staff) and above
