## Executive Summary

The AI Solutions Team is a cross-functional unit within NexusCorp's Innovation Labs organization, focused on developing and deploying AI-powered solutions for the Smart Energy Division. The team operates under a matrix structure, combining deep technical expertise with strong business acumen to deliver production-ready AI systems that drive measurable energy savings and operational efficiency.

**Team Mission:**  
*Transform building energy management through intelligent AI solutions that deliver 15-20% energy savings while advancing NexusCorp's sustainability mission.*

**Strategic Alignment:**
- Smart Energy Division business priorities
- IntelliCore Vision platform roadmap
- NexusCorp sustainability goals (carbon neutral by 2025)
- AI/ML innovation leadership in energy sector

- **Experience:** 5-7 years in ML/AI engineering

- **Responsibilities:**
  - Lead model architecture decisions

  - Production model troubleshooting

- 3-4 production models per quarter

## Team Operating Model

### Agile Framework
- **Sprint Duration:** 2 weeks
- **Ceremonies:** 
  - Daily standups (15 min)
  - Sprint planning (2 hours)
  - Sprint review (1 hour)
  - Retrospective (1 hour)
  - Backlog refinement (1 hour weekly)
- **Tools:** Jira, Confluence, GitHub, Slack

### Collaboration Practices
- **Pair Programming:** AI Engineers pair on complex algorithms
- **Code Reviews:** Mandatory for all production code
- **Knowledge Sharing:** Weekly "Lunch & Learn" sessions
- **Documentation:** Everything documented in Confluence
- **On-Call Rotation:** MLOps team handles production issues

### Communication Cadence
- **Daily:** Standup (9:00 AM SGT)
- **Weekly:** Team sync (1 hour, Mondays)
- **Bi-weekly:** Sprint ceremonies
- **Monthly:** Portfolio review with stakeholders
- **Quarterly:** Strategy planning and OKR setting
