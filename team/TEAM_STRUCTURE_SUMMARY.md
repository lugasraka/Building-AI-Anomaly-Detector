### 6. Recruitment Guide (`06_recruitment_guide.md`)
**Content:** Hiring process and interview framework

**Hiring Process (4-6 weeks):**
1. **Sourcing & Screening** (Week 1): Resume review, 5-10 candidates
2. **Initial Screens** (Week 2): HR + Hiring Manager, 30-45 min each
3. **Technical Interviews** (Weeks 3-4): Deep-dive, system design, coding
4. **Team & Culture Fit** (Week 5): Peer interviews, 2-3 team members
5. **Executive & Offer** (Week 6): VP interview, references, offer

**Interview Evaluation Framework:**
- **Technical Competency (40%):** Coding, ML knowledge, system design
- **Problem-Solving (20%):** Analytical thinking, creativity, decision making
- **Collaboration & Communication (20%):** Clarity, teamwork, stakeholder management
- **Cultural Fit & Values (20%):** Customer obsession, ownership, learning

**Scoring Rubric:** 1-5 scale, minimum 3.0 average to hire

**Diversity & Inclusion:**
- Sourcing strategies (women-in-tech, minority-serving institutions)
- Bias mitigation (structured interviews, blind resume review)
- Target: 40% women in technical roles
- Accommodations for neurodiversity and disabilities

**Key Highlights:**
- Role-specific interview workflows
- Question bank with coding exercises
- Evaluation rubrics and scorecards
- Offer negotiation guidelines
