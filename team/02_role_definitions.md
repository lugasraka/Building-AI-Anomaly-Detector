## 1. AI Solution Program Manager

### Job Summary
Lead the AI Solutions portfolio for NexusCorp's Smart Energy Division, managing a team of AI engineers and data scientists to deliver production-ready AI systems that drive energy efficiency and operational excellence. This role combines technical leadership, business strategy, and cross-functional collaboration to scale AI solutions across the IntelliCore platform.

**Reports To:** Digital Innovation Director  
**Direct Reports:** 5-6 FTEs (AI Engineers, MLOps Engineers, Data Scientist)  
**Location:** Singapore (Primary)  
**Employment Type:** Full-Time, Permanent

---

### Key Responsibilities

**Program & Portfolio Leadership (40%)**
- Define and execute the AI solution roadmap aligned with Smart Energy Division business priorities
- Manage portfolio of 3-4 concurrent AI projects with $500K+ annual budget
- Prioritize initiatives based on business impact, technical feasibility, and strategic alignment
- Establish program governance, success metrics, and reporting cadence
- Conduct quarterly business reviews with executive stakeholders

**Team Leadership & Development (30%)**
- Lead, coach, and mentor team of 5-6 AI engineers and data scientists
- Define role responsibilities, skill development paths, and career progression
- Conduct performance reviews, 1:1s, and professional development planning
- Foster culture of innovation, technical excellence, and continuous learning
- Recruit, onboard, and retain top AI talent

**Stakeholder & Customer Engagement (20%)**
- Act as primary interface between business stakeholders, product teams, and engineering
- Support sales engagements by articulating AI solution concepts and value propositions
- Contribute to proposals, solution designs, and strategic customer pursuits
- Engage with customers to validate solutions and gather feedback
- Present at industry conferences and events

**Technical & Solution Governance (10%)**
- Provide technical direction and architectural oversight for AI/ML solutions
- Ensure AI models are robust, explainable, and maintainable
- Establish best practices for model lifecycle management and MLOps
- Ensure compliance with data privacy, security, and ethical AI standards
- Stay current with AI/ML advancements and industry trends

---

### Required Qualifications

**Education:**
- Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering, Computer Science, Data Science, or related field
- Master's degree preferred (MBA, MS Engineering, or MS Data Science)

**Experience:**
- 7-10 years total experience in technology, with 5+ years in AI/ML or advanced analytics
- 3+ years leading technical teams (minimum 3-5 direct reports)
- Proven experience managing AI/ML projects from concept to production
- Experience in energy systems, building automation, or industrial IoT preferred
- Track record of delivering measurable business value through AI solutions

**Technical Skills:**
- Strong understanding of AI/ML concepts, algorithms, and model lifecycle
- Familiarity with cloud platforms (AWS/Azure/GCP) and MLOps practices
- Knowledge of data engineering, feature engineering, and model deployment
- Understanding of software engineering best practices and DevOps
- Experience with Python, SQL, and ML frameworks (TensorFlow, PyTorch, Scikit-learn)

**Business Skills:**
- Exceptional stakeholder management and communication skills
- Ability to translate business challenges into AI-enabled solutions
- Experience managing budgets, timelines, and cross-functional dependencies
- Strong presentation skills for executive and technical audiences
- Business acumen with understanding of financial metrics (ROI, NPV, TCO)

**Leadership Skills:**
- Proven ability to lead and inspire technical teams
- Experience building high-performing teams and developing talent
- Strong decision-making skills with comfort in ambiguity
- Ability to influence without authority across organizational boundaries
- Change management and organizational agility

---

### Success Metrics

**Program Delivery:**
- Portfolio ROI >150% annually
- On-time project delivery rate >90%
- Customer satisfaction (NPS) >50
- Zero critical production incidents

**Team Performance:**
- Team satisfaction score >4.0/5.0
- Employee retention rate >90%
- Time-to-productivity for new hires <60 days
- 100% of team with documented development plans

**Business Impact:**
- $2M+ annual energy savings delivered to customers
- 5+ production AI models deployed
- 3+ new customer wins attributed to AI solutions
- Recognition as thought leader (speaking engagements, publications)

---

### Compensation

**Salary Range:** SGD 180,000 - 240,000 annually  
**Bonus:** Up to 25% of base salary (performance-based)  
**Benefits:**
- Comprehensive health insurance (family included)
- Retirement contribution matching (up to 6%)
- Annual learning budget: SGD 5,000
- Stock options/equity participation
- Flexible work arrangements
- Relocation assistance (if applicable)

## 2. AI Engineer

### Job Summary
Design, develop, and deploy machine learning models for building energy optimization and anomaly detection. Work closely with MLOps engineers to productionize models and with data scientists to validate performance. This role requires strong software engineering skills combined with deep ML expertise.

**Two Levels:**
- **AI Engineer I:** Junior to Mid-level (2-4 years experience)
- **AI Engineer II:** Senior (5-7 years experience)

**Reports To:** AI Solution Program Manager  
**Location:** Singapore or Remote (APAC timezone)  
**Employment Type:** Full-Time, Permanent

---

### Key Responsibilities

**Model Development (50%)**
- Design and implement ML algorithms for anomaly detection, prediction, and optimization
- Develop feature engineering pipelines and data preprocessing workflows
- Train, validate, and tune models to achieve target performance metrics
- Research and experiment with new algorithms and techniques
- Document model architecture, assumptions, and performance characteristics

**Code & System Development (30%)**
- Write production-quality code following software engineering best practices
- Implement unit tests, integration tests, and model validation tests
- Collaborate with MLOps engineers on model deployment and serving infrastructure
- Build reusable ML components and libraries
- Conduct code reviews and maintain code quality standards

**Performance Optimization (10%)**
- Optimize model inference latency (target: <50ms p95)
- Improve model accuracy and reduce false positive rates
- Optimize memory usage and computational efficiency
- Profile and debug performance bottlenecks

**Collaboration & Documentation (10%)**
- Work with data scientists on exploratory data analysis and feature selection
- Partner with product team to understand business requirements
- Document technical designs, APIs, and operational procedures
- Present findings and recommendations to technical and business stakeholders
- Mentor junior engineers (Senior level only)

---

### Required Qualifications

**Education:**
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, Mathematics, or related field
- Master's degree preferred for Senior level

**Experience:**
- **Level I:** 2-4 years in ML engineering or data science roles
- **Level II:** 5-7 years with proven track record of production ML systems
- Experience with time-series analysis, anomaly detection, or predictive modeling
- Building automation, energy systems, or IoT experience preferred

**Technical Skills:**
- **Programming:** Python (expert), SQL (proficient), Git
- **ML Frameworks:** Scikit-learn, TensorFlow/PyTorch, PyOD, XGBoost
- **Data Processing:** Pandas, NumPy, Spark (preferred)
- **Cloud Platforms:** AWS (SageMaker, EC2, S3) or Azure (ML Studio)
- **MLOps:** Docker, model versioning, A/B testing concepts
- **Software Engineering:** Design patterns, testing, CI/CD

**Domain Knowledge:**
- Understanding of HVAC systems and building energy management
- Knowledge of statistical methods and experimental design
- Familiarity with time-series forecasting techniques
- Understanding of model interpretability and explainability

**Soft Skills:**
- Strong problem-solving and analytical thinking
- Excellent communication skills (written and verbal)
- Ability to work independently and in teams
- Attention to detail and commitment to quality
- Continuous learning mindset

---

### Success Metrics

**Technical Delivery:**
- 3-4 production models delivered per quarter
- Model inference latency <50ms (p95)
- Model accuracy >95% (precision/recall)
- Code test coverage >80%
- Zero critical bugs in production

**Code Quality:**
- Peer code review feedback score >4.0/5.0
- Technical documentation completeness 100%
- Reusable components contributed to shared libraries
- Zero security vulnerabilities in code

**Collaboration:**
- Cross-functional project satisfaction >4.0/5.0
- Knowledge sharing sessions delivered (Senior: 2+/quarter)
- Mentorship effectiveness (Senior: mentee satisfaction >4.0)

---

### Compensation

**AI Engineer I:**
- Salary: SGD 80,000 - 110,000 annually
- Bonus: Up to 15% of base salary

**AI Engineer II (Senior):**
- Salary: SGD 120,000 - 160,000 annually
- Bonus: Up to 20% of base salary

**Benefits (Both Levels):**
- Health insurance, retirement matching, learning budget

## 3. MLOps Engineer

### Job Summary
Design, build, and maintain the MLOps infrastructure that enables rapid, reliable deployment of machine learning models at scale. Bridge the gap between ML development and production operations, ensuring models are monitored, versioned, and maintained throughout their lifecycle.

**Reports To:** AI Solution Program Manager (dotted line to Platform Engineering)  
**Location:** Singapore or Remote (APAC timezone)  
**Employment Type:** Full-Time, Permanent

---

### Key Responsibilities

**MLOps Infrastructure (40%)**
- Design and implement model deployment pipelines (CI/CD for ML)
- Build and maintain model registry, versioning, and artifact management systems
- Implement automated model testing, validation, and quality gates
- Create infrastructure for A/B testing and canary deployments
- Develop monitoring systems for model performance and data drift

**Production Operations (30%)**
- Manage production ML infrastructure (Kubernetes, Docker, cloud services)
- Ensure 99.9% uptime SLA for model serving endpoints
- Implement automated scaling and load balancing
- Manage model rollbacks and emergency procedures
- Optimize infrastructure costs while maintaining performance

**Platform Development (20%)**
- Build internal tools and platforms to accelerate ML development
- Create self-service capabilities for data scientists and AI engineers
- Implement feature stores and model serving platforms
- Develop automated retraining pipelines
- Build dashboards and alerting systems

**Collaboration & Standards (10%)**
- Define MLOps best practices and standards
- Train AI engineers on production deployment procedures
- Collaborate with DevOps on infrastructure and security
- Document operational runbooks and procedures
- Participate in on-call rotation for production issues

---

### Required Qualifications

**Education:**
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field
- Certifications: AWS/Azure cloud certifications preferred

**Experience:**
- 4-6 years in software engineering, DevOps, or ML infrastructure roles
- 2+ years specifically in MLOps or ML platform engineering
- Experience with production ML systems at scale
- Kubernetes and container orchestration expertise
- Cloud infrastructure management (AWS/Azure/GCP)

**Technical Skills:**
- **Infrastructure:** Kubernetes, Docker, Terraform, Ansible
- **Cloud:** AWS (EKS, S3, Lambda, CloudWatch) or Azure (AKS, Blob, Functions)
- **CI/CD:** GitHub Actions, GitLab CI, Jenkins, or similar
- **Monitoring:** Prometheus, Grafana, ELK stack, DataDog
- **ML Serving:** Seldon, KServe, TensorFlow Serving, or custom Flask/FastAPI
- **Programming:** Python, Bash, Go (preferred)
- **Databases:** PostgreSQL, Redis, Elasticsearch

**MLOps Specific:**
- Model versioning (MLflow, DVC, or custom)
- Feature stores (Feast, Tecton, or custom)
- Data versioning and lineage tracking
- Model monitoring and drift detection
- A/B testing frameworks for ML

**Soft Skills:**
- Strong systems thinking and debugging skills
- Ability to balance speed vs. reliability
- Excellent documentation habits
- Calm under pressure (production incidents)
- Teaching and mentoring abilities

---

### Success Metrics

**Infrastructure Reliability:**
- 99.9% uptime for model serving endpoints
- Deployment frequency: 10+ per week
- Mean time to recovery (MTTR) <30 minutes
- Zero-downtime deployments 100%
- Infrastructure cost optimization (target: 20% reduction YoY)

**Developer Productivity:**
- Model deployment time <10 minutes (from commit to production)
- Self-service platform adoption >90%
- Developer satisfaction with MLOps tools >4.0/5.0
- Reduction in manual operational tasks (target: 50%)

**Operational Excellence:**
- Production incidents <2 per quarter (P1/P2)
- Automated monitoring coverage 100%
- Runbook documentation completeness 100%
- On-call response time <5 minutes

### Compensation

## Common Requirements Across All Roles

### NexusCorp Core Competencies

**All team members must demonstrate:**

1. **Customer Focus:** Understand and prioritize customer needs
2. **Innovation:** Challenge status quo and seek better solutions
3. **Collaboration:** Work effectively across teams and functions
4. **Execution:** Deliver results with quality and speed
5. **Integrity:** Act with honesty and transparency
6. **Sustainability:** Commitment to environmental and social responsibility

### Hiring Process

**Standard Process (4-6 weeks):**

1. **Application Review** (3-5 days)
   - Resume screening
   - Portfolio/code sample review (for technical roles)

2. **Phone Screen** (30 minutes)
   - HR/recruiter screening
   - Culture fit assessment
   - Salary expectations alignment

3. **Technical Interview** (60-90 minutes)
   - Coding exercise or system design (technical roles)
   - Case study or portfolio review (program manager, data scientist)
   - Problem-solving assessment

4. **Hiring Manager Interview** (45-60 minutes)
   - Deep dive on experience and achievements
   - Role-specific scenarios
   - Career aspirations alignment

5. **Team Interview** (45-60 minutes)
   - Peer interviews (2-3 team members)
   - Collaboration style assessment
   - Culture fit validation

6. **Executive Interview** (30 minutes)
   - For senior roles (Program Manager, Senior Engineers)
   - Strategic thinking assessment
   - Leadership evaluation

7. **Offer & Negotiation** (3-5 days)
   - Reference checks
   - Background verification
   - Offer presentation and negotiation

### Diversity & Inclusion Commitment

NexusCorp is committed to building diverse teams. We actively seek candidates from underrepresented groups and ensure inclusive hiring practices:

- **Gender Diversity:** Target 40% women in technical roles
- **Neurodiversity:** Welcoming non-traditional backgrounds
- **Cultural Diversity:** Global team with local representation
- **Accessibility:** Accommodations for candidates with disabilities
- **Unbiased Process:** Structured interviews with standardized rubrics
