# AI Team Onboarding Guide
## 30-60-90 Day Plan for New Hires

**Document Version:** 1.0  
**Last Updated:** February 2026  
**Purpose:** Ensure rapid integration and productivity for new AI team members

---

## Overview

This guide provides a structured onboarding experience for new hires joining the AI Solutions team. The goal is to help new team members become productive contributors within 90 days while feeling welcomed, supported, and aligned with our mission and culture.

**Onboarding Philosophy:**
- **Week 1:** Welcome and orientation (learn the basics)
- **Month 1:** Foundation building (understand systems and processes)
- **Month 2:** Active contribution (start delivering value)
- **Month 3:** Full integration (independent and impactful)

**Success Metrics:**
- Day 30: Complete onboarding checklist, understand team processes
- Day 60: Deliver first meaningful contribution (code, analysis, or design)
- Day 90: Work independently on tasks, mentor assigned

---

## Pre-Boarding (Before Day 1)

### 1 Week Before Start Date

**HR Actions:**
- [ ] Send welcome email with first-day details (time, location, parking)
- [ ] Ship laptop and equipment to new hire's address
- [ ] Create accounts: Email, Slack, GitHub, Jira, Confluence, AWS/Azure
- [ ] Prepare workspace (if office-based) or remote setup guide
- [ ] Send employee handbook and benefits information

**Manager Actions (Program Manager):**
- [ ] Schedule 1:1 welcome meeting for Day 1
- [ ] Assign onboarding buddy (peer mentor)
- [ ] Prepare 30-60-90 day plan document
- [ ] Identify first project/task for Month 1
- [ ] Send team introduction email to existing team members

**Buddy Assignment:**
- [ ] Match new hire with peer in similar role (senior level)
- [ ] Buddy commits to daily check-ins for Week 1, weekly for Month 1
- [ ] Buddy prepares "Day in the Life" walkthrough

**New Hire Preparation:**
- [ ] Complete HR paperwork (online)
- [ ] Review welcome packet and team documentation
- [ ] Set up development environment (if possible)
- [ ] Join Slack and introduce yourself to team

## Week 1: Welcome & Orientation (Days 1-5)

### Day 1: First Day Experience

**Morning (9:00 AM - 12:00 PM):**
- [ ] **9:00** - Welcome meeting with Program Manager (Raka Adrianto)
  - Team mission, values, and culture
  - Role expectations and success metrics
  - 30-60-90 day plan overview
  - Q&A and goal setting

- [ ] **10:00** - HR Onboarding Session
  - Benefits enrollment
  - Company policies and procedures
  - IT security training
  - Administrative setup

- [ ] **11:00** - Workspace Setup
  - Laptop configuration and software installation
  - Access verification (all systems)
  - Development environment setup
  - Test build and run

**Afternoon (1:00 PM - 5:00 PM):**
- [ ] **1:00** - Team Lunch (in-person or virtual)
  - Meet entire team in informal setting
  - Team history and current projects overview
  - Personal introductions and icebreakers

- [ ] **2:30** - Buddy Introduction & Pairing
  - Detailed walkthrough of buddy's role
  - Shadow buddy for remainder of day

  - Set up daily check-in schedule

- [ ] **4:00** - Documentation Review
  - Read team structure and role definitions
  - Review current project documentation
  - Explore codebase and architecture
  - Set up personal development goals

**Day 1 Deliverables:**
- [ ] All accounts created and accessible
- [ ] Development environment running
- [ ] First commit to test repository
- [ ] Schedule for Week 1 confirmed

- [ ] Identify one improvement opportunity

### Week 4: Month 1 Review

**Goals:**
- Demonstrate understanding of team processes
- Deliver meaningful contribution
- Receive feedback and adjust course

**Key Activities:**
- [ ] **Day 30 Check-in with Manager**
  - Review Month 1 accomplishments
  - 360-degree feedback collection
  - Skill assessment and gap identification

- [ ] ✅ Pass 30-day check-in with manager

- [ ] Complete technical training (certification or course)
- [ ] Deep dive into specific technology or domain area

**Goals:**
- Lead small project or feature
